Privacy Policy

re:thinc built the re:visit app to support bars and restaurants to track their customers during the COVID-19 crisis in a secure way. It is provided by "re:thinc GmbH" (re:thinc) at no cost and is intended for use as is.

If you choose to use the re:visit service you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

Who is the owner of the collected data?

Your data is stored and owned by re:thinc.

What information do we collect?

Personal Data

We may require you to provide personally identifiable information, such as:
  • E-Mail Address
In order to provide the re:visit service, it saves data that is relevant to track the visits of your customers:
  • Customer name
  • Customer phone number
  • Date and time of visit
  • Table number
  • Waiter/waitress

The requested information will be retained by re:thinc and won't be shared with other third parties.

The data will be stored encrypted so that only users with a valid password can read the data. re:thinc does not own or store this password. So only you can access the data.

The re:visit service does not collect any persistent identifiers (e.g. Google ID)

Security

Critical data like passwords and login information will be stored encrypted. Because no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is absolutely secure and reliable, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

How do we use your information?

We use your personal information only to provide the re:visit service. This includes:
  • E-Mail for login
We do not use your information for advertisement or promotional information.

What information do we share?

We do not share any of your provided data to any party outside of re:thinc.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by updating this page. These changes are effective immediately after posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy contact us at privacy@rethinc.ch.
